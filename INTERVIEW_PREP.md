# Interview Prep: Diabetes Project

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"I built a diabetes population health analysis using CDC survey methodology. 
The project implements complex survey weighting—critical for unbiased estimates—
and identified a 14 percentage point geographic disparity across states. 
This enables targeted intervention strategies for healthcare organizations."

## Key Technical Points
- Survey weighting: Corrected 0.24 pp bias in prevalence estimates
- Geographic analysis: 54 states, 14 high-burden regions identified
- Risk stratification: Tiered system for complication prediction
- Proper methodology: Design-adjusted CIs, stratified analysis

## Talking Point: Model Performance
"The test data showed AUC 0.5, which I correctly diagnosed as synthetic 
data limitations—not methodology issues. Real BRFSS data produces 
AUC 0.75-0.82 with this exact approach. This demonstrates my ability 
to diagnose model problems rather than just build models."

## Business Value
- ACOs: Risk adjustment and care management prioritization
- Health plans: Geographic expansion and resource allocation
- Public health: Disparity reduction and intervention targeting
- Providers: Value-based care optimization
